Delete Your Account

Move2Swipe is developed by The Chaos Group Consulting, LLC. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1 — From within the app:
Open Move2Swipe → Settings → Account → Sign Out. Then email us at the address below to request full data deletion.
Option 2 — By email:
Send an email to support@congrove.org with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials (email address and account ID)
  • All synced habit and completion data
  • Your blocked apps list and time bank records
  • All app preferences synced to the cloud
  • Streak milestone records

What Is Retained

  • Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Data stored locally on your device is not affected — uninstalling the app removes it

If you have questions, contact us at support@congrove.org.
